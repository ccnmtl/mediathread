How To Migrate Course Materials

Help Instructions

  1. Select a Course: Type some or all of the course name from which you wish to migrate materials. Select course from drowdown menu, then click View materials
  2. Migrate All Materials: To migrate all materials including all compositions and assignments that you have created and all items that you have imported or annotated in a course, choose "Select Complete Course" and follow directions.
  3. Migrate Assignments & Compositions: Click Select Compositions & Assignments. Scroll through the list and check materials you wish to migrate, then choose Select. Review your list then click Migrate Selected.
  4. Migrate Items: Click Select Items to Migrate. Scroll through the list and check materials you wish to migrate, then choose Select. Review your list then click Migrate Selected.

Migration Confirmation

  • Once materials have been imported, you will receive a confirmation message noting the number of materials that have successfully been migrated.
  • To import materials from another course, choose "switch course" link.
  • After materials have been migrated, they will appear as Assignments, Compositions, and/or items in your Collection in the new course.
  • Web links in migrated Assignments and Compositions may need to be updated.

Migration Details

  • Any Items and selections associated with a migrated project are also migrated.
  • Only instructor selections associated with an item are imported.
  • Student-authored compositions cannot be migrated; student-uploaded items and selections can only be migrated if they are embedded in instructor compositions.
