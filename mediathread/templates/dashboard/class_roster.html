Add by Columbia University Network ID (UNI)

About Adding Students
Normally, an instructor does not need to add users manually to a Mediathread course. Registered students are added to their affiliated courses automatically when they login to Mediathread. But, sometimes students or teaching assistants are not formally registered. This utility will allow you to add them to the course easily.

How To Add Students
To add students or teaching assistants who are not formally registered, enter a list of UNIs separated by commas. The users will be added to the course and receive an email with login instructions.


At least one UNI is required.

Invite by Email Address

About Inviting Students
An instructor can invite a student into the course by providing an email address. An invitation will be sent to the student with instructions on how to create an account and login to the Mediathread course.

How To Invite Students
To invite students, enter a list of email addresses separated by commas. The users will receive an invitation to the course via email.

Columbia University Students
Do not invite students with Columbia University Network ID via email. Their course affiliations will not be picked up correctly.

At least one email address is required.
