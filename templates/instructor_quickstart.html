QuickStart Guide:

Work in Mediathread centers around Items and Compositions:

Items are images or videos that have been Collected in MediaThread from external Sources for analyis and use in Compositions.

  • You can designate supported Sources for your course or, alternatively, import a core group of Items for analysis. See instructions in the Source Media tab.
  • All Items in MediaThread are available to all members of the class.

Compositions may contain embedded Items or Selections from those Items.

  • Any MediaThread user can create a Composition and add collaborators.
  • A Composition may be saved as a private draft, in which case it is visible only to its author(s). It may also be published and made visible to the instructor, the whole class, or the world.
  • Instructors have another publishing option: they may save a Composition as an "assignment" for the class, so responses can be tracked here, in the Instructor Dashboard.
  • Compositions may be discussed by Instructors by posting "Instructor Feedback". Class members are alerted to Feedback posted on their Composition(s).
  • Any Composition created by an instructor and made visible to the class, whether or not it is an Assignment, will appear on the Course Home Page.

Discussions are conversations about specific topics.

  • Topic-level discussions are accessible to the whole class but can only be created by instructors here in the Instructor Dashboard.
  • Once created, topic-level Discussions appear on the Class Home page.
